Whether you're a lifelong Star Wars fan or just getting into LEGO, 2026 has some incredible sets to choose from. Here are our top picks across every budget.

## Best for Display

The UCS (Ultimate Collector Series) line continues to deliver jaw-dropping builds. These are large, detailed models designed for display rather than play.

::sets 75192, 75341, 75367

## Best Value Picks

Not every great set needs to break the bank. Here are sets that deliver an outstanding piece-per-dollar ratio:

1. **Microfighters** — Small, fun builds that come with a minifigure
2. **Battle Packs** — Great army-building value
3. **Buildable vehicles** in the $30-60 range

::sets 75388, 75404

## Best for Kids

If you're shopping for a younger builder, look for sets in the 7-12 age range with play features like spring-loaded shooters and opening cockpits.

::set 75405

## How We Rank Sets

We look at several factors when ranking LEGO sets:

- **Build experience** — Is it fun and interesting to put together?
- **Display value** — Does it look great on a shelf?
- **Piece count vs. price** — Are you getting good value?
- **Community ratings** — What do other builders think?
